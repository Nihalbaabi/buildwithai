# SaveSphere AI Voice Assistant: Interaction Guide

Welcome to the SaveSphere AI Variable Assistant. Your assistant is designed to help you strictly with your electricity and water usage, providing short, clear answers without any confusing jargon or decorative icons. It responds directly to natural language queries. 

Below are the different ways you can interact with the assistant, categorized by their functioqn.

## 1. Analytics for Electricity & Water
You can now ask the assistant identically for both electricity and water analytics. By default, it answers for electricity unless you explicitly mention "water" or "liters".
*   **Current Usage:** "What is my current power usage?" or "What is my live water flow?"
*   **Daily Analytics:** "How much energy did I use today?" or "How many liters of water did I consume today?"
*   **Weekly/Monthly Analytics:** "Show my weekly consumption" or "How much water did I use this month?"
*   **Comparisons:** "Compare my usage today versus yesterday" or "Compare my monthly water consumption."

## 2. Room & Zone Comparisons
Ask the assistant to analyze usage across different regions of your home.
*   "Which room consumes the most power?"
*   "Which section uses the most water?"
*   "Show my zone distribution for electricity."

## 3. Billing & Predictions (Electricity Only)
Get insights into your current and expected electricity bills.
*   "What is my bill so far?"
*   "What is my expected electric bill for this month?"

## 4. AI Insights & Optimization
Leverage advanced AI analysis to understand your patterns and save resources.
*   **Usage Patterns:** "What is my usage pattern?" or "Analyze my behavior."
*   **Optimization & Tips:** "How can I reduce my bill?" or "Give me energy saving tips."
*   **Simulations:** "What if I reduce my usage by 20%?" or "Simulate my bill if I lower consumption."

## 5. Instant Reports
Ask for quick, summarized reports for either electricity or water.
*   "Generate my daily energy report" or "Give me my daily water report."
*   "Give me a weekly summary."
*   "Show me the monthly report for water."

## 6. Smart Room Control (Electricity)
You can command the assistant to physically turn on and off power for specific rooms. **This functionality remains fully active and working for room electricity control.**
*   "Turn off the bedroom lights."
*   "Enable power in the kitchen."
*   "Shut down all rooms."
*(Note: smart control is currently available only for electrical power controls, not water valves).*

---

### Important Notes:
1. **Strictly Focused:** The assistant is heavily optimized to only answer queries regarding your electricity and water usage. Non-domain questions (e.g., general knowledge) will be gracefully rejected.
2. **Concise Answers:** The assistant guarantees short, direct answers limited to a maximum of two sentences. This allows for quick auditory feedback.
3. **No Unnecessary Elements:** You will receive clean, text-only updates, without any emojis, warning signs, or icons cluttering your screen or speech output.
